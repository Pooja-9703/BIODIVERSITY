What is Biodiversity?

"Biodiversity" refers to the variety of life on Earth. It includes all living things—plants, animals, fungi, and microorganisms—and the ecosystems where they live. Biodiversity showcases the incredible richness of different species, genetic differences within each species, and the various environments where they exist. It's like a vast library of life forms and habitats that work together, creating a balanced and healthy environment for all living things.
